About Me

Hello! My name is Sung Hyun Hwang. I am currently pursuing a master's degree in computer science at the University of Texas at Dallas.

I graduated with a bachelor of science in electrical engineering at the University of Oklahoma. During my studies, I discovered my interest in computer engineering and programming while doing digital design with microcontrollers where I worked with C and C++ and while learning the MIPS assembly language during my computer architecture course.

Ever since, I continued to expand my knowledge of different languages and technologies while doing programming projects. In order to further expand my knowledge of computer science, I chose to continue my studies with a master's degree. My current goal is to find software engineering internships or co-op opportunities that start summer of 2021 or later.
